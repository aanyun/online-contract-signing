Click to Play – Door Adjustment (l14_p02)

Oven doors are inter-connected with a chain and turnbuckle assembly that attaches to sprockets on the lower hinge pin of each door.

Remove the lower kick panel.

Close both doors.

There should be 5 regular links and one Master link visible on each side.

If not, loosen the lock-nuts and turnbuckles, then reposition the chain.

Loosen one turnbuckle and tighten the other until the doors close correctly with the right door leading the left.

Tighten the turnbuckles until the chain is taut, but not overly tight.

Tighten all the lock-nuts to keep the doors in adjustment. Test the doors and replace the kick panel.
